Our Story

A Table Worth Gathering Around

Born from a reverence for the land and a passion for the table, The Ember Table is where Arizona's finest ingredients meet the timeless art of hospitality. Every dish tells the story of the farmer who grew it, the rancher who raised it, and the chef who honored it.

Our supper club atmosphere draws from the golden age of dining — when an evening out was an occasion, a cocktail was a ceremony, and every course was a conversation. We source within 100 miles whenever possible, changing our menu with the seasons.

Step through our doors and leave the ordinary behind. This is your table. This is your evening.

— Chef James Caldwell
