Our Story

Where Every Meal Becomes a Memory

Founded in 2012 by Chef Marcus Rivera, The Ember Table was born from a simple belief: that extraordinary food doesn't need to be complicated. It needs to be honest, soulful, and crafted with the finest ingredients our region has to offer.

Our kitchen works directly with local farms and fisheries, ensuring each plate reflects the season's best. From our signature wood-fired dishes to our handcrafted cocktails, every detail is designed to create an experience you'll want to return to.

Farm to Table

Locally sourced, seasonal ingredients from trusted partners.

Made with Love

Every dish crafted by hand with care and attention to detail.
