How Much Does a Website Cost?

Straightforward answers to the most common questions about hiring a freelance web developer and small business website costs.

How much does a small business website cost? +

A professionally built small business website typically costs between $500 and $5,000 depending on complexity. At Tayko.Dev, starter websites begin at $500 for a 5-page responsive site with a contact form. Business websites with SEO, galleries, and testimonials start at $1,200. Custom web applications with databases and dashboards start at $2,500. Unlike agencies that charge $10,000+ for basic sites, we keep prices affordable without cutting corners on quality.

How long does it take to build a website? +

A starter website (up to 5 pages) is typically delivered in 5 to 7 business days. A full business website with SEO and advanced features takes 10 to 14 days. Custom web applications with databases and authentication take 3 to 6 weeks depending on scope. We prioritize fast turnaround without sacrificing quality.

Do you work with businesses outside Arizona? +

Yes. While Tayko.Dev is based in Gilbert, Arizona, we work with small businesses across the entire United States. All communication, collaboration, and delivery happens remotely. Most of our clients never need an in-person meeting. Whether you're in New York, Texas, California, or anywhere in between, we can build your website.

What's included in the price? +

Every project includes custom design (no templates), responsive mobile-friendly development, basic SEO setup, contact form integration, revisions, and deployment to your domain. There are no hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay. We also provide a walkthrough so you know how to manage your site after launch.

Can you add features later? +

Absolutely. Every site we build is designed to grow with your business. You can start with a Starter Website and add features like booking systems, e-commerce, dashboards, or AI tools later. We also offer ongoing support plans so you always have a developer on call when you need changes.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes. 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can discuss monthly installments. We want to make professional web development accessible to every small business, regardless of budget constraints.
